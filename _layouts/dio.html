I'm a PhD student at Rutgers University where I'm lucky to have Periklis Papakonstantinou and Jaideep Vaidya as my advisors. My research interests include complexity theory, cryptography, computer security, and privacy.

In the past, I graduated from the at the National Technical University of Athens and I attended the PhD program at CUNY. I've worked at the Computer Security Lab at UCSB, Nessos and Columbia University. I've taught about discrete mathematics, computer applications, and web programming,

I live in the magnificent city of Athens in Greece.
